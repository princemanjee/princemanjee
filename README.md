Thirty years of building technology that sticks, from electrons to executive strategy. Available for senior consulting engagements and IT leadership roles with SMB, mid-market, and enterprise organizations.

I help leadership teams figure out which AI investments will actually move their business and which are theater. Then I help their people adopt those tools without throwing the existing org chart in the trash. Pragmatic, not utopian. Outcomes first.

## Currently building

Selected open source work. Each repo has its own README explaining the what and the why.

- **[AIOrchBuilder](https://github.com/princemanjee/AIOrchBuilder)**: Multi-agent orchestration framework. Production-grade scaffolding for stateful AI workflows.
- **[ClaudeMCP](https://github.com/princemanjee/ClaudeMCP)**: Multi-backend LLM gateway via the Model Context Protocol. Routes requests across providers from a single client.
- **[Engineered Prompt Library](https://github.com/princemanjee/EngineeredPromptLibrary)**: Curated, versioned prompts for production AI systems. Companion to my AI adoption consulting work.
- **[Photonic Computing](https://github.com/princemanjee/PhotonicComputing)**: Research notes on optical computing systems and their implications for the next compute substrate.
- **[Panopticon Paradigm](https://github.com/princemanjee/PanopticonParadigm)**: Thinking on observability, privacy, and the politics of data collection at scale.

Additional client and commercial work is kept private.
